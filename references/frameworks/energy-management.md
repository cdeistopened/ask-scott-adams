# Energy Management

Adams's meta-principle for all of his lifestyle and success frameworks: personal energy is the master variable. Manage energy first; everything else — career, relationships, creativity, decision-making — follows.

"Manage your personal energy first. Everything else flows from that."

## The Hierarchy

Adams prescribes a specific priority order for energy management:

1. **Sleep** — Non-negotiable foundation. Everything degrades without adequate sleep.
2. **Exercise** — Raises baseline energy for all other activities. Adams advocates morning exercise specifically for its effect on afternoon energy levels.
3. **Diet** — What you eat determines your energy curve through the day. Adams has experimented with numerous dietary approaches and consistently recommends eating for energy, not for weight loss (weight loss is a byproduct of eating for energy).
4. **Social environment** — Right people energize. Wrong people drain. This is not optional — it is a systems input.
5. **Work structure** — Do the hardest, most important thing at peak energy. Schedule draining tasks for low-energy periods.

## The Moist Robot Connection

Energy management is Adams's most concrete application of the [[moist-robot|moist robot hypothesis]] to self-improvement. If you are a biological machine, then your outputs (decisions, creativity, persistence, mood) are determined by your inputs (sleep, food, exercise, social). Trying to produce good outputs from bad inputs is fighting the machine. Fixing the inputs is programming the machine.

Adams: "Willpower is a battery that drains. Energy management is a power grid."
